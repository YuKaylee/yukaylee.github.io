How'd was the Idea of Bubble Tea Created?


In the 1980s, the owner of a small tea house in Taiwan came up with the idea during a meeting.

Chun Shui Tang, a teahouse in Taichung had recently started selling tea cold, inspired by Japanese-style iced coffee. Lin Hsiu Hui, the manager of product development at the teahouse had lost interest during a staff meeting and poured her Fen Yuan (a Taiwanese sweetened tapioca pudding) into her Assam iced tea.

After she tried her creation she added it to the menu because of how tasty it was. Soon, it was the number-one item at the teahouse and other places started copying the idea. Hence, the birth of a stunning creation, bubble tea.
