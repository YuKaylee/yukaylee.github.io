An Introduction to Bubble Tea


What is Bubble Tea?

Bubble tea is basically a drink (normally made of a tea base) mixed with sweeteners, fruit syrups, or flavourings with chewy tapioca pearls, jellies, and puddings at the bottom of the cup. Many people consider it both a drink and dessert because of it's high sugar content, however many can agree on one thing, that it's delicious.

Why "Bubble" Tea?

A common misconception surrounding Bubble tea is the origin of the name. Many people believe "bubble" comes from the tapioca pearls commonly found in the beverage. However the bubbles are actually referring to the small bubbles left at the top of the drink after it's shaken. Weird, right?

National Bubble Tea Day

April 30th was founded as a day to recognize bubble tea by Kung Fu Tea in 2018. Many bubble tea stores now celebrate this day with special discounts, offers, and promotions. In 2023 google even created a game to celebrate the occasion!
