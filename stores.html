Bubble Tea Places

In recent years, Toronto has had a growing love for bubble tea, Canada being one of the top consumers of tapioca in the world. This resulted in a large number of cafes and franchises specializing in serving these drinks. Below is a list of some of the most popular franchises in the Greater Toronto Area (GTA).

Chatime

Originating in Taiwan, Chatime is a global franchise with over 1000 store locations across the globe. Since 2005, the beloved bubble tea chain has featured freshly brewed tea and hand-made beverages in their specialty drinks. Chatime's name originates from the Chinese word for tea "Chá"(茶) and time, hence the meaning "tea time".

CoCo

In 1997 a man named Tommy Hung founded what is now known as CoCo, a bubble tea and fresh juice store. Coco's core mission, "Consistency and Continuity", is to serve customers with high quality tea and innovative drinks. CoCo's first Canadian store opened in downtown Toronto, 2014 which was the first of over 60 stores in Ontario.

Real Fruit

Real Fruit opened it's first location in Toronto's Pacific Mall 2002. Specializing in loose leaf brewed tea and espresso tea machines, Real Fruit values the freshness of fruit. All fruit drinks made by Real Fruit are created on site with real fruits and a selection of topping to choose from. The franchise's pursuit of fine craftsmanship is what allows them to stand out and continue to serve residents of the GTA.
